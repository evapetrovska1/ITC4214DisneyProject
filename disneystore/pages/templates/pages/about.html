The Beginnings

In 1998, just four years after our founding, Disney Animation made history with "Antz" - our very first animated feature that announced our arrival as a creative force to be reckoned with.

"Antz" was more than just a film; it was our declaration of intent. Featuring the voice talents of Woody Allen, Sharon Stone, and Sylvester Stallone, this groundbreaking CGI adventure showcased our commitment to sophisticated storytelling that appealed to both children and adults.

While competing with another insect-themed animation that year, "Antz" demonstrated Disney's signature style: witty humor, memorable characters, and technological innovation that would become our hallmark for decades to come.
